About Us

Compassionate Pet Care, Trusted Services — All in One Place

Meta Hush is a one-stop platform dedicated to providing top-notch care and services for pets and pet lovers. Whether you're seeking veterinary appointments, grooming, daycare, or looking to adopt a pet, Meta Hush connects you with trusted providers and ensures every pet is cared for like family.

  • Online appointments for pet grooming, daycare, and veterinary care.
  • Shop for pet essentials, grooming kits, and pet food in one place.
  • Adopt or rehome pets safely with our verified community network.

At Meta Hush, we believe pets deserve the best — and so do you. Our mission is to simplify pet parenting by offering reliable, tech-enabled services tailored to your pet’s needs. With a focus on love, health, and connection, we make sure your furry friends are happy, healthy, and in good hands.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about pet adoption, vet services, grooming, and more.

How do I adopt a pet through Meta Hush?

You can browse available pets in our adoption listings and contact the pet owner or shelter directly through our platform to start the adoption process.

Can I book vet appointments online?

Yes, Meta Hush allows you to schedule appointments with partnered veterinary clinics directly from the website for your convenience.

What grooming services are available?

We offer a range of grooming options including bathing, hair trimming, nail clipping, and specialized treatments depending on your pet's needs.

Is pet insurance available through Meta Hush?

Yes, we provide access to various pet insurance plans to help cover veterinary costs and emergencies.

Can I list my pet for adoption on the platform?

Absolutely! You can create a profile and list your pet along with photos and details to help find them a new loving home.

What safety measures are in place for pet transactions?

We verify all listings and encourage direct communication with verified users. Our team monitors activities to ensure safe and responsible pet adoption and services.
